## What You Pay For

While AnyCowork is free, you'll need:

### AI Provider API Keys

You bring your own API keys from:

- **Anthropic** - Pay-as-you-go pricing
- **OpenAI** - Pay-as-you-go pricing  
- **Google** - Free tier available, then pay-as-you-go

Typical costs: $0.01 - $0.10 per conversation depending on model and length.

### Infrastructure (Optional)

If you want to run AnyCowork on cloud infrastructure:

- **VPS/Cloud Server** - $5-50/month (DigitalOcean, AWS, etc.)
- **Domain Name** - $10-15/year (optional)

Or run it completely free on your local machine!

## Why Free?

We believe AI should be accessible to everyone. By making AnyCowork open-source:

- **Transparency** - See exactly how it works
- **Privacy** - Your data stays with you
- **Flexibility** - Modify it to fit your needs
- **Community** - Build together with others
- **Innovation** - No barriers to experimentation
